Clarity

Eliminate ambiguity. Enable people to see,
understand, and act with confidence.

  • What just happened
  • Where you are
  • What you can do
  • What will happen when you do it

Flexibility

Design something that looks good
in every situation.

  • Don't reinvent the wheel
  • Typography: ensure WCAG AA compliance
  • Consider all the possible content: no content, short content, long content, ugly content
  • Account for all the devices and resolutions

Familiarity

Our brain love seeing simple
and familiar things.

  • Use common navigation patterns
  • Icons. When in doubt, replace an icon with text
  • Micro copy: use simple, popular verbs
  • Use common color codes: red, orange, green, and blue

Efficiency

Streamline and optimize workflows
using a simple and functional core interface.

  • Anticipate needs to help people work better, smarter, and faster
  • Make sure the users complete their main task in the most efficient way and never lose the result of their work

Consistency & Structure

Focus on UI to facilitate discoverability
and ease of use.

  • Introduce strong visual hierarchy
  • Align everything along the grid
  • Keep navigation consistent
  • Reuse elements
